Third Year Project Oral Report

Individual Examiners Report



At the third year project Oral the examiners are examining oral skills (20 per cent) and the quality of the implementation (80 per cent). The student should give a brief overview of the project (the assumption here is that the examiners have not read the final paper), give a demonstration of the implementation and should be ready to answer any questions on the project.


It is the students responsibility to make sure that a working version is ready for demonstration at the Oral. If a demonstration is not available then document the reason (and do not give a mark for the quality of implementation). The student is given this one chance to demonstrate their implementation and further demonstrations must not be arranged except in exceptional circumstance such as the ITS network not being available during the time of the Oral (please inform Stephen McGough if the Oral is rescheduled).


Note the Assessment Criteria to be used are in the enclosed document.
